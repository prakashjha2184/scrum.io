What is an AI Scrum Master?

An Artificial Intelligence-powered tool that emulates the functions of a traditional Scrum Master, helping teams facilitate effective meetings, summarize decisions and actions, and keep their board up to date.

Like a human Scrum Master, an AI Scrum Master doesn't manage the team but empowers the team to achieve its goals and deliver high-quality products.
